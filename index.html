About Snap Happy Events – Greenville SC’s Premier Photo Booth Company

Snap Happy Events (SHE) is your go-to source for unforgettable photo experiences in Greenville SC and the Upstate. Our mission is to make every event vibrant, fun, and unforgettable. From interactive 360 photo booths to custom Glam Package experiences, we bring professional photo entertainment directly to your party, wedding, or corporate event.
